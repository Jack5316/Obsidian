## Workflows

1. **Capture** - Dump everything into [[00 - Inbox/Start Here|Inbox]]
2. **Process** - Clarify, tag, and link inbox items
3. **Organize** - File into Projects / Areas / Resources
4. **Distill** - Extract key ideas into permanent notes in `Atlas/`
5. **Express** - Use knowledge to create, build, and share
6. **Review** - Weekly review using [[Weekly Review]] template
